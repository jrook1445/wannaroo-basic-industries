Copper production notes:

Accepted cargo is stockpiled (no limits) and processed every production tick.

Copper requires both Copper Ore and Oil on a production tick: 4 CORE + 2 OIL_ => 5 COPR

Nothing is produced from Copper Ore alone. Copper Ore is stockpiled if Oil is not available.

If Oil but no Copper Ore: 4 OIL_ => 1 RFPR, 2 PETR, 1 PLST as usual.

near town >= 1200 | 500,

30-40 tiles from town center
